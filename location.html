Location


Seriti is situated 4 hours from Gauteng and 40 minutes from Nelspruit Kruger International Airport(MIA). For private aircraft there is an airfield at Malelane and Komatiport, both of which are a 20 minute drive.

SA Airlink offers daily flights from Durban, Cape Town and Johannesburg to MIA.

Seriti is perfectly located to explore the splendours of Swaziland or the Lowveld and is a mere 2 hours from some of the finest beaches of Africa.
